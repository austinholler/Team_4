  • equalizerWhy City Pulse?

    City Pulse aggregates data freely available on Meetup.com's API. We use this data to paint a picture of the 'pulse' of a city.

  • supervisor_account Who are we?

    We are students at the University of Colorado Boulder. This is a project for CSCI 4830/7000(Big Data), taught by professor Greenstreet.

  • businessSupported Cities
